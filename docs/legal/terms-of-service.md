## 1. Service Description

Patchwork is a mutual aid coordination platform built on the AT Protocol. It
connects people who need help with volunteers and organisations in their
community. The platform provides tools for posting aid requests, discovering
nearby needs, coordinating volunteer responses, and managing community
resources across a federated network.

Patchwork is **not** a professional services provider, emergency service, or
charity. It is infrastructure for peer-to-peer mutual aid coordination.
